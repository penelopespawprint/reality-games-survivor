How to Play

Rank your top castaways from 1-24 based on who you think will score the most points. Lock in your choices by March 3, 2026 at 5pm PST (after the first episode airs).

Each player will receive two players in snake draft order with a random turn order.

Each week, choose which of your 2 castaways to "play" for that episode. Your picked castaway earns (or loses) points based on what happens during the episode.

The player with the most total points at the end of the season wins!
